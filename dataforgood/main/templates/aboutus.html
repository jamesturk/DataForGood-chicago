Who We Are

DataForGood Chicago is a web application designed to assist small to medium-sized nonprofit organizations in Chicago in utilizing data effectively for decision-making and resource management. Our platform offers a centralized solution to access, analyze, and visualize socioeconomic data from multiple sources.

Our Mission

To democratize access to data-driven insights, enabling nonprofit organizations to harness the power of data for social good and drive positive change in their communities.

Key Features

  • Integrated Mapping with ArcGIS Online
  • Customizable Data Dashboards
  • Automated Insights and Memo Generation
